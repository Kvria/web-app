WHAT IS YOUR GHANIAN 'AKAN' NAME?

Do you know your Akan name?...

The Akan people in Ghana used to name their children according to day of the week they were born and their gender.

A good example is of the first Ghanian president Kwame Nkurumah who's name clearly shows that he was born on a Saturday.

Here are some of the names according to the gender;

Did you know that through this, one can discover
that the former UN secretary general, Kofi Anan, was born on a Friday?

    Male names

  • Sunday -Kwasi
  • Monday -Kwadwo
  • Tuesday -Kwabena
  • Wednesday-Kwaku
  • Thursday -Kwo
  • Friday -Kofi
  • Saturday -Kwame

    Female names

  • Sunday -Akosua
  • Monday -Adwoaa
  • Tuesday -Abenaa
  • Wednesday-Akua
  • Thursday -Yaa
  • Friday -Afua
  • Saturday -Ama
